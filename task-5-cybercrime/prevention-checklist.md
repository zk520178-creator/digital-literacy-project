# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP or passwords with anyone
- Always check website URLs before entering details
- Avoid clicking on unknown links
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking transactions
- Regularly update your apps and devices
- Verify emails and messages before responding

## Financial Safety Tips
- Double-check UPI IDs before payment
- Do not accept unknown payment requests

## Report Cybercrime
- Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in
